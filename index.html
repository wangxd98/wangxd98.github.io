Xiaodong Wang （王晓东）

        Vice President of Fujian University of Technology

        Professor, School of Mathematics and Computer Science

        Fujian University of Technology

     
    Research interests:

    • Computer algorithm design and algorithm evaluation
    • Large-scale problem-solving algorithms and data structures based on computer networks and information security
    • Information visualization technology
    • Geometric computing
    • Parallel and distributed algorithm design
    • Computational complexity theory

    Major roles and activities:

    • Mainly engaged in research in the field of computer algorithm design and analysis.
    • Led and completed projects such as the National Natural Science Foundation.
    • Won one Second Prize of National Science and Technology.
    • In 1990, went to Max Planck Institutes as a visiting scholar.
    • In 1997, was exceptionally promoted to professor at Fuzhou University.
    • Has served as dean of the School of Mathematics and Computer Science at Fuzhou University.
    • Vice president of Quanzhou Normal University.
    • Vice president of Fujian University of Technology.
